AutoWater

In this project, I designed and built a solar-powered automatic plant waterer from scratch using Arduino and TinkerCAD. The device uses moisture sensors to determine when to water the plant, weight sensors to determine when the tank needs more water, and a bluetooth device to notify the user when the water needed to be refilled. This would ensure that they receive the right amount of water without human intervention.
